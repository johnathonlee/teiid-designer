Designer Views

Views are dockable windows which present data from your models or your modeling session in various forms. Some views support particular Editors and their content is dependent on workspace selection. This section summarizes most of the views used and available in Designer.

Below is a short summary of Designer views. The full list is presented in the main menu's View > Show View dialog.
